Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Minimal To-Do Checklist ("the App") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the App.

Information Collection and Use

The App is designed to work fully offline and does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

  • No account creation is required.
  • No personal data is requested.
  • No data is sent to external servers.

Local Data Storage

All tasks, checklists, and settings you create are stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device unless you manually back it up or uninstall the App.

Third-Party Services

The App may display advertisements using Google AdMob. AdMob may collect limited, non-personally identifiable information as required to serve ads, in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy.

Minimal To-Do Checklist itself does not control or access any data collected by third-party advertising services.

Children’s Privacy

This App does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. Since no personal data is collected, the App is safe for general audiences.

Security

Because the App operates offline and stores data locally, your information remains under your control on your device.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, you may contact the developer via the app listing on Google Play.
