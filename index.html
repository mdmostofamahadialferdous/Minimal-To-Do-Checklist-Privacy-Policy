Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Minimal To-Do Checklist (“the App”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the App.

Information Collection and Use

The App is designed to function fully offline. It does not collect, store, share, or transmit any personal information.

  • No user accounts or login are required.
  • No personal or sensitive data is requested.
  • No data is transmitted to any server.

Local Data Storage

All to-do items, checklists, and app preferences are stored locally on your device only. This data remains on your device unless you uninstall the App or manually back up your device.

Third-Party Services

The App may display advertisements using Google AdMob. AdMob may collect limited non-personal information (such as device identifiers) to serve ads, in accordance with Google’s Privacy Policy.

The developer does not collect, access, or control any data gathered by third-party advertising services.

Children’s Privacy

This App does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. As no personal data is collected, the App is suitable for general audiences.

Security

Because the App operates offline and stores data locally, your information remains under your control on your device.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
