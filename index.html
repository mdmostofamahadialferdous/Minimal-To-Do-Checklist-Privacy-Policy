Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Minimal To-Do Checklist (“the App”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the App.

Information Collection and Use

The App is designed to work fully offline and does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

  • No user registration or login is required.
  • No personal data is requested.
  • No data is sent to external servers.

Local Data Storage

All tasks, checklists, and preferences are stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device unless you choose to delete the app or manually back up your device.

Third-Party Services

The App may display advertisements using Google AdMob. AdMob may collect limited, non-personal information (such as device identifiers) to provide and improve ads, in accordance with Google’s Privacy Policy.

The developer does not access, store, or control any data collected by third-party advertising services.

Children’s Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. Since no personal data is collected, the App is safe for general audiences.

Security

Because the App operates offline and stores data locally on your device, your information remains under your control.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer through the Google Play Store listing.
